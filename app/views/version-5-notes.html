Version 5 – tested in Belfast on 21 February 2020

In this version we tested the one-at-a-time design in more depth.

We tested with 6 PIP healthcare professionals at Capita in Belfast.

Screens tested

  • Start
  • Later in the day

Differences from version 4

We changed the following for this test:

  • users pick up the next case as they go - they reach a ‘done’ page where they are prompted to review another case
  • dashboard is arranged into cards for each type of task with a header, a total of cases, a prompt to take the next task, and a list of any referrals they've saved
  • saved referrals appear as a table within each card and show the type of referral, how long its saved for, and why it's saved
